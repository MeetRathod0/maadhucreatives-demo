About Us

Our dedication and passion we have grown into the most trusted model-making solution

Welcome to Maadhu Creatives, where creativity meets craftsmanship!

Since our founding in November 2016, we have been committed to providing innovative model-making services that bring ideas to life. Based in Mumbai, India, and serving clients worldwide, Maadhu Creatives has become the go-to solution for anyone looking to transform their concepts into tangible, visually stunning models.

From architectural scale models to film props and industrial prototypes, we combine cutting-edge technology with artisanal craftsmanship to deliver models that not only meet but exceed expectations. We take pride in turning our clients’ complex ideas into inspiring realities, enabling them to visualize their dreams with clarity and precision.

Our logo features the "Ninja Star" or "Shuriken," a common name for a Ninja Warrior weapon.

The logo’s hue and texture stand for the peace of creativity and our weapon, paper, respectively. While we use the same qualities in all of our creative outputs, the logo’s illustration, texture, and colour complement our philosophy.

Ninja warriors are renowned for their dedication, quickness, accuracy, and the development of their weapons for maximum impact.

What Sets Us Apart:

What sets Maadhu Creatives apart is not just our expertise but our passion for innovation. We embrace challenges and turn them into opportunities to showcase our creativity. Whether it’s architectural models, industrial prototypes, or artistic representations, we approach each project with the same level of enthusiasm and dedication. Our clients trust us for our attention to detail, commitment to quality, and timely delivery.
