Production Manager Job in Mumbai

Join Maadhu Creatives

Job Description

Location: Mumbai

Experience: 2 to 5 years

Employment Type: Full-time

Maadhu Creatives is a dynamic and innovative organization committed to delivering high-quality creative solutions. We are seeking an experienced Procurement & Vendor Management professional to join our Mumbai team and support our operations.

Key Responsibilities:

1. Material Planning & Requisition Handling

  • Receive and review Material Requisitions (MR) from production, model-making, painting, landscaping, electrical, and other departments
  • Verify specifications, quantities, project references, and urgency levels
  • Clarify discrepancies with the requesting department before proceeding

2. Vendor Sourcing & Management

  • Maintain and update the Approved Vendor List (AVL)
  • Identify new vendors for cost optimization, better quality, or urgent/specialized materials
  • Evaluate vendors based on price, quality, delivery timelines, and after-sales support

3. Quotation & Negotiation

  • Obtain quotations (minimum 2–3 where applicable)
  • Prepare Comparative Statements for management approval
  • Negotiate prices, lead times, and payment terms to achieve the best cost-quality balance

4. Purchase Order (PO) Management

  • Prepare and issue POs with accurate specifications, quantity, rates, delivery dates, and quality requirements
  • Ensure PO approvals follow the company authority matrix
  • Track delivery progress until materials are received

5. Market & Local Purchases

  • Handle emergency or local purchases when required
  • Ensure bills are in the company name with correct GST details
  • Verify material meets approved specifications before handing over to the Store Executive

6. Coordination with Store & Accounts

  • Inform Store Executive prior to material delivery
  • Share PO and bill copies with Accounts
  • Resolve discrepancies related to quantity, rates, or vendor billing issues

7. Vendor Performance & Cost Control

  • Monitor vendor performance regularly
  • Recommend cost-saving solutions without compromising quality
  • Support management with procurement data and reports

Role Limitations:

  • Cannot issue materials to production
  • Cannot maintain or alter stock records
  • Cannot bypass store processes

Required Skills & Qualifications:

  • Experience in procurement, vendor management, or a related field
  • Strong organizational, negotiation, and analytical skills
  • Ability to manage multiple vendors and projects efficiently
  • Knowledge of material specifications, purchase orders, and vendor evaluation

Benefits:

  • Competitive salary
  • Career growth opportunities
  • Stable employment in a creative and dynamic environment
  • Exposure to diverse manufacturing and model-making projects
